About This Study

This research study is being conducted as part of a Masters dissertation in Software Engineering at the University of Oxford. The study aims to investigate the cognitive load of using an AI assistant to read and manage newsletters. Your participation will contribute valuable insights to my understanding of Human-Computer Interactions.


The study involves interviews, cognitive testing as well as using an application and is expected to take approximately four weeks to complete (two weeks of testing and one interview or survey the week before and the week after the testing).


All data collected will be kept strictly confidential and used solely for research purposes. Participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time.

Participation involves...

Qualitative Study

If you are interested in sharing your thoughts and experiences about reading newsletters, are curious about taking part in a cognitive test and want to try an new application.


  • Two interviews
  • Observation session
  • Cognitive testing
  • Discussion about newsletter habits
  • Using an application for two weeks

Quantitative Study

If you are short on time but would like to test a new application and answer surveys.


  • Two surveys
  • Using an application for two weeks

Eligibility Criteria

Inclusion Criteria

You may be eligible to participate if you meet the following criteria:

You are 18 years old or over
You are fluent in English
You have access to a MacOS computer and Google Chrome
Familiar with coding and able to use a terminal
You are subscribed to newsletters that you struggle to get around to reading

OR

You are subscribed to newsletters that you read and manage easily

Want to Participate?

If you meet the eligibility criteria and are interested in contributing to this research, I'd love to hear from you!
