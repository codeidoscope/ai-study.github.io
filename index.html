This research study is being conducted as part of a Masters dissertation in Software Engineering at the University of Oxford. The study aims to investigate the cognitive load of using an AI assistant to read and manage newsletters. Your participation will contribute valuable insights to my understanding of Human-Computer Interactions.

The study involves interviews, cognitive testing and using an application and is expected to take approximately three weeks to complete.

All data collected will be kept strictly confidential and used solely for research purposes. Participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time.

Group 1: Qualitative Participants

I am looking for individuals who have the time to test an application for two weeks as well as take part in two 2h-long interviews that will include an observation exercise, a cognitive load exercise and discussions around email newsletters reading and management habits.

Group 2: Quantitative Participants

I am looking for individuals who have the time to test an application for two weeks but may not have the time to take part in the interviews. You will be given access to an application that will collect usage data for two weeks, but will not be required to take part in the interviews.
